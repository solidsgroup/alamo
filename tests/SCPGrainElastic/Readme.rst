
This test simulates coupled solid-fluid interaction (SFI) of SCP grain deflagration.
The initial phi condition (phi 0,1 = HTPB,AP) is from a processed image of an SCP microstructure.
The below figues show the inital condition and transient response of the grain during deflagration.

1) Inital phi showing difference between AP and HTPB
2) Regression of the solid/fluid phase field, eta
3) x direction stress (top) and strain (bottom) during regression
4) Velocity magnitude of the fluid phase
